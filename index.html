Where Is Samantha Her From?

I am Hmong. Hmong has a long history of going from one place to another and we do not currently have a country of our own- but we do still manage to get together when times call for it.

A couple generations back, my grandma and grandpa used to live in Laos and Thailand. When the Vietnam War hit, we sided with the United States because of conflicts.

Unfortunately we lost- but we were granted a pass to America and here I am, part of the first American-born Hmong generation.

We don't have any books- but oral stories say we originated from China.
